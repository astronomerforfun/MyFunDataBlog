If you're considering learning R start with Dplyr and work your way from there!

November 22, 2018

Objective:

I wanted to give a quick once over of dplyr in less than 10 minutes. This is an intro that will get you started. After watching the video pull down the Cheat sheet off of R Studio’s Website and study it. Practice for two weeks and if you like what you see give it another week and then move full time into R for analytics. If you’re not into it its ok too as there are other languages, like Python, etc. that you can look into. Good luck and I hope this helps get you started.
